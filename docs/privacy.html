Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Meals Planner ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. User-Provided Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • User-generated content (recipes, meal plans)
  • Photos (optional, for recipes)

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics
  • Performance data

3. Advertising-Related Data

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Usage patterns for ad personalization

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To store and manage your recipes and meal plans
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about the app

Third-Party Services

1. Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and data storage. Their privacy policy can be found at https://supabase.com/privacy.

2. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob for advertising. Their privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can opt-out of personalized advertising
  • You can request to delete your account and associated data
  • You can export your data
  • You can modify app permissions in your device settings

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure authentication methods.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
