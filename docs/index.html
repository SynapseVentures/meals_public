Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new recipe?

Tap the "+" button in the recipes tab and fill in your recipe details. You can add ingredients, steps, and even photos to make your recipes complete.

How do I share recipes with my household?

Once you've created a household, all members will automatically have access to shared recipes. You can manage household members in the settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that this action cannot be undone and will delete all your data.
